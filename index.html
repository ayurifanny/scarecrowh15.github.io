Preventing the Birth of Ultron

If you're a fan of Marvel Cinematic Universe, you must know a supervillain called Ultron. It's an super AI that made by Tony Stark with help from Dr. Banner (or we know him as Hulk) with aim to protect the earth These two geeks are very passionate about tech and they have a vision to make very powerful machine to protect the earth from every threat that comes from earth or outside the earth like space. We all know, that their machine turns to be a malfunctioned machine that interprets 'saving humanity' means 'destroying humanity' Ultron 'thinks' that if there are no life on earth, there will be no threat comes to earth

While AI like Ultron or maybe J.A.R.V.I.S is still not really exist in this earth, we still should be encountering some of other form of AI in our daily life Let's say our current AI might not thinking to destroy the earth or make chaos to the entire world, but we still have to be worried of danger that could possibly emerge from AI.

Issues of responsibility

AI or we call it as Artificial Intelligence is very interesting topic to discussed with in this era. Everybody is so excited to have this kind of super technology and eager to give it a try. While this powerfull technology is open for everybody, some problems could be arise too. Some people would use AI to create a 'bad manner' machine. The intention may vary, from just for fun or even a personal bad intention to harm other people.

Ethical Challenge

It's one of the major problem for Artificial Intelligence. AI is trained based on data that is given. It cannot determines the correctness of the data. The misuse of the data could lead to ethical violations. If the algorithm is made to reflect racism or terrorism, it will give the out just exactly as it trained to. It will ignore the correctness or quality of data given, thus it's could lead to several major problems.

Security

This is an critical aspect in AI. When a man wants to create a machine that can behave like human or even outsmart human, some of security issues may appear. Like when people try to create an autonomous car, they have to face the consequences that it could possibly lead to malfunction. We cannot fully trust the machine that we create because if some errors occured, it could always be a harmful things to us.

The main things to prevent a creation like Ultron appear in our reality is to consider all the possibilities of our creation and to have a firm consideration before creating some AI machine. Try to think about the aspects in humanity and make sure that our machine don't violates our humanity value.
